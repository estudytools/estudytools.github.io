Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Student Tools Suite

Are all tools really free? ▼

Yes! All 29 tools in Student Tools Suite are completely free to use. We believe every student should have access to powerful study tools regardless of their financial situation. No hidden charges, no premium versions, just free tools to help you succeed.

Do I need to create an account? ▼

No account needed! Many of our tools work instantly without any registration. Your data is stored locally on your device for complete privacy. You maintain full control over your information at all times.

Is my data private and secure? ▼

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy. Most data is stored locally on your device, and we never sell or share your personal information. All connections are secure, and we comply with international privacy standards.

Which tools work offline? ▼

Most of our core tools are designed to work offline. The Flashcard Generator, Concept Map Builder, Timer, and many others function perfectly without an internet connection. Check individual tool pages for specific offline capabilities.

Can I use these tools on mobile? ▼

Yes! All our tools are designed to be responsive and work great on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Use them on the go to study anywhere, anytime.

How do I export my data? ▼

You can export data from most tools directly through the tool interface. We support common formats like PDF, CSV, and JSON to ensure your data is portable and useful wherever you need it.

Do you have plans to add more tools? ▼

Yes! We're constantly working on new tools based on student feedback. Follow our updates to stay informed about new features and tools being added to the suite.

How can I provide feedback or report issues? ▼

We'd love to hear from you! Visit our contact page to submit feedback, report bugs, or suggest new features. Your input helps us make Student Tools Suite better for everyone.

Are there tutorials or guides available? ▼

Yes! Each tool includes built-in instructions and our blog has detailed guides on how to use each tool effectively. Check our study tips section for comprehensive tutorials.
